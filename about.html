The Founder

The Mind Behind Mora Digital

Mohanarangan N is a technology entrepreneur, developer, and digital strategist based in Puducherry, India. He founded Mora Digital Automations with a singular mission — to make premium digital products accessible to businesses of every size.

With hands-on expertise in web development, mobile applications, and software engineering, Mohanarangan leads every project with a detail-oriented, client-first approach. His philosophy is simple: build things that actually work, look great, and deliver real value.

Before founding Mora Digital, Mohanarangan worked across multiple technology domains — sharpening his skills in full-stack development, UI/UX design, and digital marketing. Today, he leads a growing team that serves clients across India and internationally.
