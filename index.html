Mohanarangan N is a technology entrepreneur and the founder of Mora Digital Automations, based in Puducherry, India. Mohanarangan founded Mora Digital Automations with a mission to make high-quality digital services accessible to businesses of all sizes — combining deep technical expertise with sharp design thinking to build products that perform and impress.
