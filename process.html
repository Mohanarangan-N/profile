How We Work

Our Process

A clear, proven 6-step process that takes your idea from a brief conversation to a fully launched digital product — on time, on budget, with zero surprises.

Step by Step

From Idea to Launch

01
🔍
Day 1–2
Discovery & Briefing

Every great product starts with deep understanding. In this first phase, we schedule a free consultation call with you to understand your business, your goals, your target audience, and your budget. We ask the right questions — so nothing gets missed later.

You don't need to have everything figured out. Our job in this phase is to help you clarify your vision and turn it into a concrete project brief that guides everything that follows.

What You Get

Free 30-minute consultation call
Written project brief and scope of work
Clear project timeline and milestones
Transparent, itemised cost estimate
Zoom / Google Meet WhatsApp Notion
02
📐
Day 3–7
Strategy & Planning

Before writing a single line of code, we plan everything. We define the project architecture, choose the right technology stack for your specific needs, map out the user journey, and create a detailed development roadmap.

This phase prevents expensive mistakes later. A well-planned project is a project that gets delivered on time — every time.

What You Get

Technology stack recommendation with reasoning
Site map or app flow diagram
Development roadmap with weekly milestones
Project kickoff confirmation
Figma Notion Trello Miro
03
🎨
Week 1–2
UI/UX Design

Our designers create the visual blueprint of your product. We start with wireframes — simple black-and-white layouts that define the structure and user flow. Once approved, we move to high-fidelity mockups with your brand colours, fonts, and imagery.

You review and approve every screen before development begins. No surprises. No "that's not what I imagined" moments after launch.

What You Get

Wireframes for all key pages / screens
High-fidelity UI mockups in Figma
Interactive prototype for review
Design approval before coding starts
Figma Adobe XD Illustrator
04
💻
Week 2–6
Development

This is where ideas become reality. Our developers write clean, well-structured, and well-documented code that brings your approved designs to life. We work in sprints and share progress updates with you every week — so you always know exactly where your project stands.

We build with scalability in mind. Your product won't just work on launch day — it will continue to perform as your users and business grow.

What You Get

Weekly progress updates and live preview links
Clean, documented, maintainable codebase
Mobile-responsive across all devices
Integrated CMS, APIs, and third-party tools
React / Next.js Node.js Flutter Python GitHub
05
🧪
Week 6–7
Testing & Quality Assurance

We don't ship anything we wouldn't be proud to show off. Before launch, your product goes through rigorous testing across multiple devices, screen sizes, and browsers. We test every feature, every button, every form, and every edge case.

You also get a full client review period — test everything yourself, send us your feedback, and we fix every issue before the product goes live.

What You Get

Cross-browser and cross-device testing
Performance and speed optimisation
Security checks and vulnerability scan
Full client review and revision round
Chrome DevTools Lighthouse BrowserStack Postman
06
🚀
Week 7–8
Launch & Ongoing Support

Go time. We handle the full deployment — setting up hosting, connecting your domain, configuring SSL, and making sure everything runs perfectly on day one. We monitor the launch and stay on call for 48 hours after go-live.

After launch, we don't disappear. All plans include post-launch support, and we offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your product running at its best as your business evolves.

What You Get

Full deployment to your hosting / domain
SSL certificate and security setup
Google Analytics and Search Console setup
Training on how to manage your product
Post-launch support included in all plans
Netlify / Vercel GitHub Pages Google Analytics Search Console

Process FAQ

Questions About How We Work

How involved do I need to be during the project? +

As involved as you want to be. We can work with as little as a weekly check-in or as much as daily updates — whatever suits you. At minimum we need you available for design approvals and the final review round.

What if I want to change something mid-project? +

Minor changes within scope are handled at no extra cost. Significant scope changes are discussed transparently and quoted fairly before implementation. We never spring surprise costs on you.

Do you work on weekends? +

Our core hours are Monday to Saturday, 9AM to 7PM IST. For urgent issues post-launch, we are available via WhatsApp and respond as quickly as possible.

What happens after my project is launched? +

All plans include post-launch support. We offer monthly maintenance packages for ongoing updates, security patches, and new feature additions. We are a long-term partner, not a one-time vendor.
