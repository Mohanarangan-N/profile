Common Questions

Services FAQ

How long does a website take to build? +

A standard 5-page website takes 1–2 weeks. Custom web apps or e-commerce stores take 4–8 weeks depending on features. We give you a clear timeline before we start.

Do you provide support after the project is delivered? +

Yes. All plans include post-launch support. We offer maintenance packages so your product stays updated, secure, and running perfectly at all times.

Can I update my website content myself? +

Absolutely. We build with CMS platforms like WordPress when appropriate so you can update text, images, and pages without any coding knowledge.

Do you work with clients outside Puducherry? +

Yes — we work with clients across India and internationally. Our communication and delivery process is fully remote-friendly via WhatsApp, Zoom, and email.

What do I need to provide to get started? +

Just tell us about your business, your goals, your target audience, any reference websites you like, and your budget. We handle everything from there with a free consultation call.
